7 sept 24

today was one day among many, every day fills every few.
a few moments shared with people, and some moments spent alone.


i woke up late, it mustve been around lunchtime cause my roommates were making a lot of noise in the hallway. i waited a second for them to leave so i could take a pee and make myself some coffee.
after an hour or so my mom called me because she was going to a yoga seminar in utrecht (which is where i am living right now).
she offered to bring some of my stuff from utrecht back to my hometown of Zoetermeer. this has to happen because im moving out of my current apartment next month.
she picked me up and we drove to her seminar so i could take her car back to my apartment for free parking while she was doing her thing. the seminar lasted about 3 hours which was great cause i had to clean my bathroom. after picking her up, we went to pick up the stuff im bringing back to Zoetermeer. stuff like sir Michael Perry Allen's 4 track reel to reel which i havent been using enough to cary it around with me though i want to so bad! but dont worry, itll get a nice little home in zoetermeer.

thanks for reading my cage.blog, love and byebye
