yello! Im Dylan Hokstam.

im a musician, creative coder, composer, and artist.
I like to make electronic instruments and design gadgets and sites and stuff.

on the 29th of november 2024 I released my first album under the alias Cage.Log.
this was possible through the help of a few different very special people you can read about it here.





please let me know if the loading animation just scared your grandma.

did you know you can drag the windows displayed on screen? pretty nifty..

this is where more content is coming in

im working on a lot of different projects at the moment, i love working on MlemRecords with friends, im working on some music projects by myself also on the side which will be coming up soon also. ive got a few hours of music ready-ish but im thinking about a few different inquaries.

ive got the first version of my first audio vst working, the (working title) timeLooper0.5. if you try it out and notice some features youd like to see added, please contact me at info.cagedotlog@gmail.com

find it on github at git@github.com:CDyLhox/timeLooper2.git

otherwise: mixing, mastering, and letting go. these are not easy. when do you finish a project? its so much easier to make a new project instead, which is fine. but in time this creates an issue though. one i have often nowadays, because at this moment im 2 years ahead of the music im releasing and have released and i only feel the gap growing larger. this will work itself out though.
im working on a smaller project which has nothing really to do with the regular cagedotlog sound. but i might still release it as is, have you listened to any new music recently?

Wacko_ IS OUT


The album follows a narrative of “weirdifaction” —a bright sonic world that gradually unveils a creeping sense of absurdity. Wacko_ is the first album written, and recorded, by Cage.log. It is an IndieTronica/Art- pop album that blends Jazz, Funk, IDM, and Ambient influences.
